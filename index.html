Simultaneous motion & image recovery

  • Prior motion reconstruction: Data, code and results for prior- and motion-based reconstruction (PRIMOR) method for respiratory gated CT. Motion artefact is removed by simultaneous motion and image reconstruction. Publication.
  • Motion estimation CS MRI: This repository contains MATLAB code for B-spline-based compressed sensing (SPLICS). SPLICS generalizes spatiotemporal total variation (ST-TV) by modelling the motion between consecutive frames into the reconstruction framework. Publication.

Compressed sensing

  • Compressed-sensing diffusion lung MRI: data, code and results for a novel compressed sensing method presented in the paper Incorporation of prior knowledge of the signal behavior into the reconstruction to accelerate the acquisition of MR diffusion data. Publication.
  • Split-Bregman ST-TV MRI: Split Bregman spatiotemporal total variation for cardiac-cine MRI. Publication.
  • Split-Bregman PICCS fMRI: Prior image compressed sensing (PICCS) leveraging the Split Bregman for fMRI. Publication.
  • Efficient ART Split-Breman: MATLAB code for an iterative reconstruction method that alternates a computationally efficient linear solver (ART) with a fast denoising step based on the Split Bregman formulation. Publication.

Inverse problems - Optimization - Regularization

  • Efficient ART Split-Breman: MATLAB code for an iterative reconstruction method that alternates a computationally efficient linear solver (ART) with a fast denoising step based on the Split Bregman formulation. Publication.
  • Split-Bregman TV Jacobian: Constrained total variation image reconstruction problem solved using the Split Bregman formulation where the Jacobian is provided as a matrix.
  • Publications by topic: Publications on inverse problems, optimization and regularization.

Modelling - FEM

  • Anisotropic modelling in EIT: Publication on the influence of anisotropic modelling in electrical impedance tomography of brain function.
  • Influence of absorption and scattering on f-DOT: Publication on the influence of absorption and scattering on the quantification of fluorescence diffuse optical tomography.
  • Validation of FEM: Publication on the validation of a finite element solution for anisotropic EIT.
